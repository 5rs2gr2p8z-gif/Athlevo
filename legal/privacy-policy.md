# Privacy Policy

Last updated: [DATE]

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Athlevo.

Athlevo is a web-based AI endurance coaching platform designed to help athletes improve their training through personalized coaching guidance, performance insights, and educational support.

This Privacy Policy explains how Athlevo collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your personal information when you use our website, applications, and related services.

Athlevo is initially operated in the Philippines and aims to comply with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173), its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and applicable guidance issued by the National Privacy Commission (NPC).

By using Athlevo, you acknowledge that you have read this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use Athlevo, we may collect the following information.

### Account Information

* Name
* Email address
* Authentication information
* Account preferences

### Athlete Profile Information

You may voluntarily provide information including:

* Age
* Sex
* Height
* Weight
* Training experience
* Race goals
* Preferred events
* Performance history

### Training and Coaching Information

You may provide:

* Workout history
* Training schedules
* Workout feedback
* Session ratings
* Readiness check-ins
* Sleep quality
* Energy level
* Stress level
* Recovery status
* Pain reports
* Soreness reports
* Injury information
* Notes submitted to coaches or the AI

### AI Conversation Data

Athlevo stores conversations between you and the AI coach to:

* provide coaching responses
* improve continuity between sessions
* remember athlete preferences where applicable
* troubleshoot service issues
* improve platform performance

### Connected Fitness Data

If you choose to connect external services, Athlevo may receive activity information from those services.

Current integration:

* Strava

Future integrations may include:

* Garmin
* COROS
* Apple Health
* WHOOP
* Polar
* other compatible platforms

The information received depends on the permissions you grant to each provider.

### Technical Information

We may automatically collect:

* IP address
* Browser type
* Device type
* Operating system
* Log files
* Error reports
* Session identifiers
* Cookie or local storage identifiers
* Usage analytics

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

* create and manage your account
* authenticate users
* personalize coaching recommendations
* generate AI coaching responses
* monitor training progress
* calculate readiness and recovery metrics
* synchronize connected fitness data
* provide customer support
* improve our services
* detect abuse or security issues
* comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

## 4. AI Services

Athlevo uses artificial intelligence providers, including OpenAI and potentially other providers, to generate coaching responses.

Information you submit during coaching conversations may be processed by these providers to generate responses.

AI-generated recommendations are intended to support training decisions and should not replace professional medical advice.

## 5. Third-Party Service Providers

Athlevo relies on third-party providers to operate the platform.

These may include:

* Supabase (authentication and database)
* Vercel (hosting)
* OpenAI or another AI provider
* Strava
* Future wearable integrations
* Future payment processors such as PayMongo

These providers process information only as necessary to provide their services under their own privacy and security practices.

## 6. International Data Processing

Some of our technology providers may process or store information outside the Philippines.

By using Athlevo, you understand that your information may be transferred to countries that have different data protection laws.

Where appropriate, Athlevo will take reasonable steps to ensure that personal information receives an appropriate level of protection.

## 7. Cookies and Local Storage

Athlevo may use:

* cookies
* browser local storage
* authentication tokens
* similar technologies

These help:

* keep you signed in
* remember preferences
* improve performance
* maintain security

You may adjust browser settings to limit cookies, although some features may no longer function correctly.

## 8. Data Retention

Athlevo retains information only for as long as reasonably necessary to:

* provide services
* comply with legal obligations
* resolve disputes
* maintain security
* improve coaching continuity

Deleted accounts may have certain information retained for legitimate legal, accounting, fraud prevention, or security purposes where permitted by law.

Backup systems may require additional time before information is permanently removed.

## 9. Your Rights

Subject to applicable Philippine law, you may request:

* access to your personal information
* correction of inaccurate information
* deletion where appropriate
* withdrawal of consent where processing is based on consent
* information about how your data is processed
* a copy of certain personal information where applicable

Requests may be submitted through:

Dean Castro, Email: support@athlevo.org.com

Identity verification may be required before fulfilling requests.

## 10. Security

Athlevo uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect personal information.

However, no internet transmission or electronic storage system is completely secure.

While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 11. Children's Privacy

Athlevo is intended only for users who meet the minimum age specified in our Terms of Service.

If we learn that personal information has been collected contrary to applicable law or our policies, we may remove that information and suspend the account.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

Athlevo may update this Privacy Policy periodically.

Material changes may be communicated through the website or application.

The "Last updated" date will indicate the latest revision.
